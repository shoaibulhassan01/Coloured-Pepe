Coloured Pepe

Coloured Pepe (CP) is a meme-based cryptocurrency inspired by the renowned Pepe the Frog meme. It aims to bring fun, vibrancy, and community-driven growth to the world of decentralized finance. Celebrating the internet's love for memes, Coloured Pepe ensures that everyone can own a piece of internet history while investing in a light-hearted digital currency.
